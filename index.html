Interactive Linear Regression

Formula

The linear regression model is:

$$ y = a + bx $$

where:

  • $a$ is the intercept
  • $b$ is the slope

The slope can be computed by

$$ b = \frac{n\sum xy - (\sum x)(\sum y)} {n\sum x^2 - (\sum x)^2} $$

and the intercept by

$$ a = \frac{\sum y - b\sum x}{n}. $$
